Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

  • What is Netflix all about?

    Netflix is a streaming platform that offers a variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, documentaries on internet-connected devices.
    You can watch your favourites as many times you want, whenever you want with ZERO ads. – For a very low monthly subscription. There's always something new to discover. Latest TV shows , Webseries, movies are added every week!

  • How much does NetFlix cost?

    Watch Netflix on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or on any streaming devices, all for one fixed monthly subscription fee.
    Plans range from 150INR to 2000INR a month. No extra costs. (Inclusive of GST)

  • Where can I watch?

    Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your Netflix account to watch instantly on the web at netflix.com from any internet connected device (smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, any streaming media players.
    You can also download your favorite shows with iOS, Android, or Windows app. Use downloads option to watch without an internet connection. Take Netflix with you anywhere.

  • How do I cancel the subscription?

    Netflix is flexible. You can easily cancel your account online in less than two steps. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime and anywhere.

  • What can I watch on Netflix?

    Netflix has an extensive library of great films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, award winning Netflix originals, and more. Watch as many times you want, anytime you want.
